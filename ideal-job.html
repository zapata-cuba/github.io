Description of the position

This senior position involves taking ownership of
a hybrid environment and delivering customized solutions as per business
requirements, all while working with all the relevant stake holders to ensure

Experienced required

While the requirements are there for On-premise
enterprise infrastructure, a must have experience is to have worked with Cloud
technologies to be able to deliver IaaS, PaaS and SaaS solutions.

Other required experiences are around app packaging,
virtualization and deployment as part of a CI/CD pipeline.

MY skills

Over the years I have gained a good level of
experience in managing an on-premise enterprise infrastructure around
Windows.  I’ve also had experience in
managing a company WAN network (Firewalls, switches, VPN)

I have a good level of experience in providing
front end support with VMware Virtual Infrastructure, this also includes
ThinApp.

I have excellent communication skills and a huge appetite to get into working with Cloud technologies.

job.

Citrix Certification Associate – Virtualization (CCA-V)

AWS Certified Solutions Architect – Associate
