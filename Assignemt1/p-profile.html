Personal Profile

I took my online Myers-Briggs test with 16Personalities
and according to its results, my personality type is Advocate – Personality
INFJ-A / INFJ-T

As per my online learning style, I took a test
with Training.com.au and according to its results I am a visual learner

For my 3rd test, I took a creativity
test with Psychologies and according to its results I am enriched by creativity.  I believe this to be
somewhat true, as I have been a artist/musician for several years, and moving into IT has been a career change for me.

I found all these three tests to have some truthfulness of the real me, I realized this while doing responding some of the questions
without hesitation.  As per my
Myers-Briggs personality test, I believe this reaffirms to me that I need to be a bit more assertive and firmer with my opinions.  I do feel sometimes that I may come across as a “soft” type of personality, however inside I feel very different, so I
believe I need to work on better projecting myself.  This not necessarily a bad thing, I believe I
tend to be laid back in most situations, however, I feel this helps me deal greatly with stressful situations.
